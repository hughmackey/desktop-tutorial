Galen Cassidy Peria, also known as Duke Aeroplane, is a song-finder, wordsmith, filmmaker, student of the cards, histrionic, and excitable drinker. Based in New Orleans, Louisiana, where he makes a highly-regarded crawfish etouffee, plays piano for his cat, lights candles to St. Jude, and screams at televised baseball, the Duke perennially snowbirds to parts north in the summer months as much of his mail still arrives at various addresses in the greater Burlington area.
